Vikings

Storyline


The adventures of a Ragnar Lothbrok: the greatest hero of his age.
The series tells the saga of Ragnar's band of Viking brothers and his family
as he rises to become King of the Viking tribes. As well as being a fearless warrior,
Ragnar embodies the Norse traditions of devotion to the gods:
legend has it that he was a direct descendant of Odin, the god of war and warriors.



Episode 1: Rites of Passage


Ragnar goes on a trip of initiation with his son.
Meanwhile, he thinks he has finally found a way to sail ships to the west.
However, his beliefs are seen as insane so he chooses to go against the law.





Episode 2:Wrath of the Northmen


The stage is set for the first journey west by Ragnar Lothbrok
as he gathers a crew willing to risk their lives to travel into the unknown.
